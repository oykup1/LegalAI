Service Agreement

This Service Agreement (“Agreement”) is made between Alpha Corp (“Provider”) and Beta LLC (“Client”) on July 1, 2025.

1. Services
Provider agrees to deliver IT consulting services, including system maintenance and troubleshooting, for a period of six (6) months.

2. Payment
Client shall pay Provider a monthly fee of $2,000, payable within 15 days of receipt of invoice.

3. Termination
Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days’ written notice. Upon termination, Client shall pay for services rendered up to the termination date.

4. Confidentiality
Both parties agree to keep all confidential information disclosed under this Agreement strictly private and not to disclose it to third parties.
